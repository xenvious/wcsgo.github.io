Basic Rules

The Basics

  1. We try to be family friendly. You may swear to a civil extent but only in RP. Swearing in OOC will be punished with a quick slap and a polite mention to behave. RP swearing/racism must be done tastefully. You cannot run around calling everyone a giant evil rutabaga just for the fun of it.
  2. Instaban offenses: Spawn block, prop spam, toolgun spam, mass extremely random RDM (rooftop sniping everyone etc).
  3. Insta kick offenses are normally just for extreme vulgarity in OOC/homophobia etc. We don't care if you tickle men, women, ponies or rutabagas just don't be obnoxious and offensive in OOC.
  4. You may not prop climb, however you may use RP entities as ladders. Such as shipments, becki-boxes or even printers!
  5. Wire & E2 must be used to aid RP in realistic manners. Fading doors and the usual stuff. Any E2 deemed to be abusive may be removed.
  6. Please have a reason for demoting people.
  7. Vote yes unless you have a reason to vote no, it's not nice to be cruel.
  8. 'I'm building' is not a reason to propblock, leaving your base open should make people see you have no valuables. Prop blocks may be removed by admins.
  9. Do not Fading Door Abuse ? This refers to when you use a button, wire etc.. to quickly close a fading door again after being cracked. Fading doors must stay open at least 8 seconds (see below for more details)
  10. Using specifics prop or materials to get one way windows is only allowed when covering an existing MAP window.
  11. Votes, sales and any form of RP involvement has to be done through /advert and not through OOC.
  12. Do not use any form of command (eg: kill/suicide, change job) to avoid a RP situation.
  13. This should fall under common sense, but physgun beams should be visible.
  14. Having illegal items makes all your items classified as contriband. IE: You have two printers and an ammo shipment. Police can take everything, not just the printers.
  15. Printers may not be bought/sold/traded between players.
  16. There is no need to /advert raid.
  17. You must actually interact with someone to rob them. No keybinds.
  18. StupidWeasel (STEAM_0:1:24400282) is a meta creature and inhabits a different plane of existance to normal players. He does not exist in a roleplay sense & can (most of the time) be safely ignored.
  19. Finally and most importantly, play nice & have fun!

Death, Combat & NLR

Death, Combat & RDM

  1. Never use lethal force first. Killing someone is a last resort, not the first and best option.
  2. There should never be a reason for combat on newly spawned players at spawn.
  3. Never RDM (Random Death Match). This refers to killing randomly without a legitimate roleplay reason.
  4. While raiding you should only shoot those who are ARMED or you have good reason to believe they will harm you.
  5. You must have reason to kill an unarmed person. Examples would be: RP Robbery, blocking path of escape, refusal to leave property, taking hostile action - 911 calls or being a spy etc, official hitman tasks or a well though out hobo revolt against the king hobo!
  6. Unarmed is classified as equipped, however during raiding it's safe to assume certain jobs are armed.
  7. Classification of 'helping a friend'. In RP terms your friends are your gang members, employers etc. You may protect them, defend them, avenge them all to a degree. Remember the aim is to have fun. You may not go on a revenge rampage due to a guy you play with on the odd second Tuesday being on just because you want to RDM someone.
  8. Inciting RDM. Hobo revolutions, killing all cops or your average anti Swedish campaigns. These are not RP, they are excuses to RDM so don't do it. Protests, revolutions, hobos united etc is all allowed but in a proper fashion and not a /job Cop Killa kinda of thing, although in a decent situation a peaceful protest can turn violent etc, but that's not the same as simply shooting people.
  9. Pistols & Shotguns are, by default, legal. Cops may not arrest a player for simply carrying these weapons, and must ask them to put the weapon away. Players who refuse to do so after a resonable period may be arrested. Players may also be arrested for using a legal weapon threateningly (discharging it in the street, for example) <-This rule may be altered by an active mayor->
  10. You're not required to advertise the fact you are raiding, if you do law enforcement officers are welcome to /warrant you immediately (and it may be considered baiting, depending on the circumstances)
  11. Intentionally killing players with a printer ("Printer Bombing") is treated just like attacking them any other weapon. If you've no reason to kill them (ie: they're innocent or unarmed), it'll be treated as if you've broken the applicable rule above (ie: RDM).
  12. However throwing a burning printer out of your base to save your life is common sense and is not RDM.

New Life Rule (NLR)

  1. NLR is an acronym for New Life Rule. New life rule is, simply stated, the rule that states if you die, you must not return to the area of your death for 5 minutes.
  2. If you must pass the place of your death then do not interact with the area or the people involved. Simply walk past the property.
  3. You can not simply stand outside it watching and waiting and then BAM jump in after exactly 5 minutes and murder them.
  4. You can however after 5 minutes insist they leave, if they do not after a few minutes you may murder them at will :)
  5. You may ask the raiders if the raid has expired. Since this is not a pure RP situation you may ask in OOC, however it is preferred to ask in a PM.
  6. If you accidentally kill yourself building then you may return to the place of death.
  7. If you die of accidental death DURING an RP event, such as a base raid, then NLR does apply.
  8. If you break NLR you may be punished by an admin with either a Slay, Kick or 60 minute ban. Repeat offenders may receive harsher punishments.

Job Rules & Guidelines

Criminals

Kidnapper:

  1. You are a predator, and the darkened alleys in town are your hunting ground. Kidnap people to molest, torment, and ransom!
  2. You are also a loner, and a weirdo. You do not work well with others or operate in a gang.
  3. Do not use this job as an excuse to RDM. You may only kill your victims if they attempt to escape, or do not follow your orders, or their ransom isn't paid.
  4. It is also allowed to kill your hostages if you grow tired of their company, but only if you have had them held captive for a minimum of 10 minutes.
  5. You may make a base, but you must be out of it and actively sneaking around and looking suspicious.
  6. You may not base with anyone else: even other kidnappers, hired bodyguards or mercs.
  7. You may not raid.
  8. You may not be part of a gang.
  9. Do not use this job to just knock out and RDM people.
  10. You may not target the same individual over and over. Once you've kidnapped someone, you must have a different target afterwards for at least 10 minutes.
  11. Do not bludgeon someone just to steal their weapons. This will be treated with an instant ban.
  12. You may not take part in raids.
  13. You must attempt to avoid being seen by police. This is RP, and you are a strange person who kidnaps people. You are not BFFs with the Mayor.
  14. You may be hired by certain jobs to obtain targets for them.

Pickpocket:

  1. Hands to yourself, sneak thief!
  2. You are not allowed to mug. With skills like yours, why would you ever need to? You must RP your job. No running up to people and demanding they wait for you to pickpocket them. Be an opportunist.
  3. You may not raid.
  4. You may only base with other pickpockets.
  5. You may not be part of a gang.
  6. You must RP your job. Failure to do so will result in a ban, and perhaps even a permban from using this job.

Black Market Dealer:

  1. You are the secret weapons dealer. Be subtle, and don't publicly advertise your shady dealings.
  2. This job is illegal, and primarily deals in remote, secluded alleyways. If you have a store, it must be very subtly disguised. You sacrifice security for subtlety.
  3. A BMD store may have only one keypad door to be used on a small safe. You cannot use base/raid defences. (shooting windows, raid entrances, etc.)
  4. You can hire a bodyguard, but make sure that they are as disguised as your store.
  5. You may not use automatic gun stores.
  6. You may not be part of a gang, though you may be "bought out" by one and refuse to sell to anyone else. Buyout charge must be over 50k.
  7. You SHOULD own a secret shop. A banana stand that has guns in the back room. A bar that literally sells shots. Stuff of that sort.
  8. You may not raid, ever.
  9. You may not mug people.
  10. You can be a mobile seller, but remember, the police are everywhere and they do not have to /wanted you before arrest if they see you selling to someone.

Meth Cook:

  1. You're no Heisenberg, but you're damn close.
  2. Your job is illegal and if you are caught by the police, or the DEA if you're very unlucky, you are going to face hard time.
  3. You can employ a bodyguard, or hide a merc firm for protection, but you may not work with other cooks or within a gang building.
  4. Do not use E2 chips to accelerate the process of making meth. The job earns a lot of filthy money, shortcuts aren't needed.
  5. Almost everything you do is illegal.
  6. You cannot base with a gang, but you can be part of one.
  7. You are required to tell any gang you join your base coordinates. You do not have to grant them access to your keypads or doors.

Thief/Master Thief:

  1. It's your job to perform cunning stunts! Wow others with your criminal finesse. You can work in a gang, or as a lone wolf. Players can be mugged for no more than $1,000 but they can be told to drop items of value that you know them to be carrying.
  2. You may raid.
  3. You may be part of a gang.
  4. You may mug.
  5. You can only be part of gang using the Mob Boss/Gangster gangs. No Resistance.
  6. You may freelance for the Resistance, but you may not base with them. You will be hired per job.
  7. Everything you do must be at least attempted to be sneaky. No buying body armor and shooting everyone dead before forcing your way into a base. That being said, if a group is hiding in their base, you may strong-arm your way in if they don't come out of hiding for a while.
  8. You may break into the PD, but you may not "take over" and declare you are in charge of the PD. Shutting down the government is a job for the Resistance.
  9. No kidnapping anyone. That is a job for the kidnapper.

Hitman:

  1. The serial killer that accepts credit cards! And the alternative to diplomacy when your neighbour refuses to trim his hedge.
  2. Contracts must be taken out with a valid reason. We aren't saying you need to ask your customers for a reason, but if you suspect metagaming or NLR to be afoot then don't accept the hit.
  3. Accepting hits on the same person on repeat is breaking NLR.
  4. You are a lone wolf. You do not ally with others, employ guards, or operate in gangs.
  5. All of your weapons are illegal. If a cop uses a weapon checker on you, you are screwed.
  6. You may set up a base, but you are to have no extended stays in said base. Think of it more of a resting/planning area.
  7. You may not be part of a gang.
  8. Do not ask people to put a hit out on someone you want to kill. This is metagaming and RDM.
  9. Do not advert. This includes "Need help with someone" or other things that don't directly say you're a hitman. Murderers don't put out want ads on Craigslist.
  10. Do not use this job as an excuse to RDM.
  11. Remember, changing jobs and having someone put a revenge hit out on someone breaks about 5 rules and will lead to an instant ban. Just a friendly reminder.

Gangster:

  2. As with the thief, you may join gangs, raid, mug, and commit crimes.

  5. You may mug.
  6. If you join a gang and align with the Mob Boss, you must all have the same /job name. IE a Mob Boss and 3 Gangsters all do "/job Ridelovers."
  7. If you are aligned with a Mob Boss, he is in charge. Think of it the same way Police Chief is in charge of Police. You do what he says.
  8. Most things you do are illegal, so direct confrontation with the police is to be avoided as best you can, unless you're breaking into the PD or some other RP reason that contact with police is unavoidable.
  9. You may not raid and "take over" the PD. That is a job for the Resistance.
  10. You may have a thief in your gang, but only one. This includes Master Thief. If a thief is in your gang, he is still required to follow the RP rules of his class.

Civilians

You may have a small keypad protected safe, but no other security defences. You are allowed to work as a civilian employee in other people's businesses, but cannot defend the business or your employer in the event of a raid or attack.

  3. You may not mug.
